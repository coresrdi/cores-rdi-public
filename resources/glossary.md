## Key Terms

- **Soulavin** — The CORES proprietary TBM system designed for Martian subsurface excavation
- **Basalt Glass Lining** — Structural liner produced in-situ from Martian basalt rock, providing structural integrity and radiation shielding
- **Annular Gap** — The space between the excavated tunnel wall and the installed liner ring, filled with insulation and structural layers
- **Phase 0** — Initial robotic deployment and infrastructure setup before crew arrival
- **Phase 1** — First crewed operations with minimal crew
- **Phase 2** — Expanded colony operations with growing population
- **Phase -1** — Lunar proving ground and staging operations
- **Tunnel Loop** — The primary circular tunnel network forming the colony backbone
- **Launch Window** — Earth-Mars transfer opportunity occurring approximately every 26 months
- **Conjunction Blackout** — Period when Mars is behind the Sun relative to Earth, blocking communications
