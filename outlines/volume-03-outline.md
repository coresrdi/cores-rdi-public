# Volume 03 — TBM & Tunneling Systems

**Status:** Active — Most technically advanced volume

## Description

The Soulavin tunnel boring machine system: mechanical architecture, drilling operations, tunnel geometry, liner installation, excavation performance, and technology readiness roadmap. This is the core engineering volume of the project.

## Chapter Outline

- Chapter 1 — Introduction to Martian Tunneling
- Chapter 2 — Mars Geological Conditions for Tunneling
- Chapter 3 — Machine Architecture and Design
- Chapter 4 — Tunnel Geometry and Cross-Section
- Chapter 5 — Liner System and Tunnel Structural Integrity
- Chapter 6 — Excavation Performance and Scaling
- Chapter 7 — Challenges and Open Engineering Problems
- Chapter 8 — Technology Readiness and Development Roadmap

> **Note:** Volume 03 and Volume 04 form a complementary pair. Vol.03 covers the machine and its drilling operations. Vol.04 covers the materials and systems built by/after the machine.
