# CORES RDI — Red Dawn Initiative

> **Colonization & Orbital Research for Excavation — Planetary Subterrane Tunneling System**

---

## About the Project

CORES Red Dawn Initiative is an open research and engineering project focused on developing a viable **planetary tunneling system** for Mars colonization. The project explores subsurface habitat construction using in-situ resources, with emphasis on basalt glass lining technology and autonomous tunnel boring machines (TBMs).

This public repository contains outlines, summaries, and selected resources from the CORES RDI book series — a 10-volume technical reference covering all aspects of the mission architecture.

---

## The 10 Volumes

| # | Title | Description |
|---|-------|-------------|
| 01 | **Mission Foundation & Vision** | Vision, objectives, history and philosophy of CORES |
| 02 | **Mars Environment & Science** | Martian environment, geology, atmosphere, reference data |
| 03 | **TBM & Tunneling Systems** | Soulavin system, TBM design, drilling geology, tunnel geometry |
| 04 | **ISRU, Materials & Construction** | In-situ materials, basalt glass lining, insulation, joints |
| 05 | **Energy Systems & Power Generation** | Power generation, storage, and distribution systems |
| 06 | **Habitat Design & Life Support** | Habitat design, ECLSS, life support systems |
| 07 | **Transportation, Logistics & Supply Chain** | Starship logistics, supply chain, ISRU transition |
| 08 | **Communication & Information Systems** | Telecommunications, networks, information systems |
| 09 | **Human Factors & Crew Operations** | Human factors, crew operations, training |
| 10 | **Financial Planning & Sustainability** | Financial planning, economic model, sustainability |

> **Note:** Volumes 03 and 04 form a complementary pair — Vol.03 covers the machine and drilling operations, Vol.04 covers materials and systems built by/after the machine.

## Classification Policy

CORES RDI uses a two-level classification system:

- **Level A — Public** : General information, outlines, summaries, and non-sensitive technical overviews. Available in this repository.
- **Level B — Confidential** : Detailed technical content, proprietary calculations, design decisions, and full chapter content. Maintained in the private repository.
